Meet Your Audiologist

Dr. Melissa Palmer, Au.D.

Dr. Melissa Palmer, Owner and Audiologist, and her team provide personalized hearing care for adults and families in Rocky Mount, Nashville, Wilson, Tarboro, Spring Hope, Roanoke Rapids, and surrounding areas across eastern North Carolina.

Our approach is intentional and warm. We slow down, we listen, and we build a plan around the life you want to live, not a one-size-fits-all script. Whether this is your first hearing test or your tenth set of hearing aids, you will be seen by a Doctor of Audiology, every single visit.
