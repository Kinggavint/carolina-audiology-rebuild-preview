Common Questions

Answers to what patients ask first

How do I know if I need a hearing evaluation?

If you find yourself turning up the TV, asking people to repeat themselves, or struggling to follow conversations in noisy places, a diagnostic hearing evaluation can give you a clear answer. Many adults wait years before getting tested. There is no benefit to waiting.

Do you take my insurance?

We accept Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, UnitedHealthcare, Humana, Aetna. If your plan is not listed, give us a call and we will help you understand your options.

Which hearing technology brands do you fit?

We work primarily with Oticon and ReSound because of their sound quality, reliability, and connectivity. Your fitting is matched to your hearing and your lifestyle, not to a single product.
