About Carolina Audiology Associates

Locally owned, doctor-led hearing care in Rocky Mount since 1988.

Meet Your Audiologist

Dr. Melissa Palmer, Au.D.

Dr. Melissa Palmer is the Owner and Audiologist at Carolina Audiology Associates. She leads a team that provides personalized hearing care for adults and families in Rocky Mount, Nashville, Wilson, Tarboro, Spring Hope, Roanoke Rapids, and surrounding communities across eastern North Carolina.

Her approach is intentional, warm, and educational. Patients leave appointments understanding what is happening with their hearing and why each recommendation makes sense. Family members are welcomed into the conversation, because hearing loss is rarely a one-person experience.

Helping you hear what matters most.
