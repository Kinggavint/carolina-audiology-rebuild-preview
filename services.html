Our Hearing Services

Doctor-led care for every step of your hearing journey, from your first evaluation to ongoing follow-up.

01   Diagnostic Care

Comprehensive Hearing Evaluations

Your hearing tells a story, and we take the time to listen to all of it. A comprehensive hearing evaluation at Carolina Audiology Associates includes a careful health history, otoscopy, pure-tone testing, speech testing, and middle-ear measurements. Every evaluation is performed and reviewed by a Doctor of Audiology.

You leave knowing exactly where your hearing stands today and what your options are, with no pressure and no sales tactics. If you do not need hearing aids, we will tell you so.

02   Treatment

Hearing Aid Fittings & Follow-Up Care

When advanced hearing technology is the right path, we fit each device precisely to your ears, your lifestyle, and the listening environments you live in. We work with ReSound, Oticon, Signia, Starkey, Widex, and Phonak, with strong service and warranty support behind every fitting.

Follow-up is not an afterthought here. Your fitting includes a full schedule of follow-up visits so we can fine-tune the settings as your brain adjusts and as your life changes. You will not be handed a box and sent home.

03   Service

Hearing Aid Repair

Hearing aids work hard. Wax, moisture, and daily wear take a toll, and even the best devices sometimes need attention. We clean, troubleshoot, and repair most major hearing aid brands in our Rocky Mount office, including aids that were not originally purchased from us.

For repairs that require the manufacturer, we coordinate the entire process for you, so you are not left chasing service tickets.

04   Ear Health

Earwax Removal

Impacted earwax is one of the most common and most overlooked causes of muffled hearing. Our team performs safe, gentle earwax removal under direct visualization, using techniques chosen for the health and comfort of your ear canal.

Many patients are surprised by how much clearer everything sounds the moment a blockage is removed.

05   Prevention

Custom Ear Protection

The best hearing aid is the one you never need. Custom-molded ear protection is designed around your unique ear anatomy and the specific environment you are protecting against, whether that is a worksite, a hunting blind, a concert stage, or a swimming pool.

Musicians, hunters, swimmers, motorcyclists, and industrial workers all benefit from protection that actually fits, seals, and stays in place.

06   Family Support

Family-Centered Counseling

Hearing loss is rarely just one person's issue. Spouses, children, and grandchildren all feel it. We invite loved ones into the conversation and teach communication strategies that take pressure off the person with hearing loss and improve connection for everyone at the table.
